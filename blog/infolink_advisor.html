Senior Peer Advisor: InfoLink

I am very excited to say that I was accepted to be a Senior Peer Advisor at Infolink during the winter term. This is such an incredible experience where I can learn more skills that I will hopefully apply in my future career path in Software Development. With that said, I am also eager to apply the skills and knowledge that I have learned previously from my courses, research work, and customer service job.

Overview

What will I do?
From the UASU Website, the overview of what an Infolink Senior Peer Advisor does is as follows:

InfoLink Senior Peer Advisor focus is to provide quality customer service to the campus community. InfoLink currently has three partner locations: Student Life Central, InfoLink ONEcard Service Centre, International Services Centre Desk.

What do I hope to learn?
To provide accurate information to clients, I have to think and problem solve quickly and efficiently. I believe that it is imperative to be able to work with diverse group of people, so I hope to improve my communication skills whether it be with coworkers or clients. Further, I hope to solidify my work ethics to help my fellow students and other people needing campus help.

Remarks

Please check out the InfoLink Webpage for more information! Until next time.
