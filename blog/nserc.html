NSERC USRA

This early afternoon, I got an email from the Computing Science undergraduate advisors of the University of Alberta stating that I received an NSERC Undergraduate Student Research Award. I am extremely lucky to have this opportunity to be a Research Assistant under the supervision of Dr Guohui Lin for the months of May to August of 2021.

Project

Project Title: Mapping Combination of Genetic Mutations in Influenza A Viruses to Antigenicity

What will I do?
I will be assisting a graduate student in dataset collection, normalization, and integration. As well, I will aid in implementing statistics, machine algorithms, and numerical experiments. Using the results obtained from the aforementioned procedures, I will help with result discussion and manuscript preparation.

Remarks

I am excited to apply the knowledge I have garnered from my computing courses to a real biological application, especially during the Covid-19 pandemic.
